What is Unlimited Blade Works?

Unlimited Blade Works is one of the three routes of the original Fate/Stay Night visual novel. This route focuses on the main heroine in this route who is Rin Tohsaka, who makes a small cameo in Fate/Zero before the 4th Holy Grail War happened. This route also focuses on Rin's servant, Archer, where it goes more into detail about his backstory.

The point of view in this route is seen mostly in Rin's point of view at the start but it then transitions into Shirou's point of view as the story goes on.
