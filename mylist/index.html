The first gacha game I have ever been invested in. The story is the main reason why I am so invested into this game and collecting characters is another reason as well.

A game similar to Team Fortress 2. It is also a class based shooter but instead of customizable weapons for nine characters, it has an array of characters with different sets of abilities.
Genshin Impact
Another gacha game that I got invested in. The character collection and the grinding for powerful artifacts is what got me invested into this game.
Apex Legends
My favorite battle royale game. The fast paced combat and the different weapons make this game fun. The characters are also likable with their own sets of abilities.
Persona 5
My favorite JRPG. This game is in my opinion, the most stylish looking JRPG game I have seen. The music is amazing, the combat is also amazing.
Persona 5 Strikers
A spinoff of my favorite JRPG game. In this game it becomes a Dynasty Warrior like game. The combat and music is absolutely amazing alike the first game.

The first game I have ever been invested in. This open world game allows you to be creative and interact with other people as well.
Muse Dash
The other rhythm game that I play. This game is a mix of aesthethically pleasing art and fun gameplay.
