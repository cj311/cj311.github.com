osu!

This is my favorite rhythm game to play. There are four different game modes but I normally play osu! standard. Since all the maps are customly made by other players, most songs that I like will have a map for the song. This allows me to play my favorite songs and practice my hand eye coordination as well. I play this normally to warm up but I've grown to actually play the game by itself without the thought of warming up. Game's Wikipedia

Fate/Grand Order

This is my most played gacha game. At first I played this just for the collection and because I'm in the Fate fandom, but now I got hooked into the story as well. This game is basically collecting servants and using them in battle. I normally play this to just pass time. Game's Wikipedia

4

Overwatch

This game is similar to Team Fortress 2 except that it has many more heroes without customizable loadouts. This game unlike Team Fortress 2 encourages counterpicking against heroes and more teamplay. I play this game always with my friends but rarely when alone since it requires alot of teamwork. Game's Wikipedia

5

Apex Legends

This game is my favorite battle royale game. It combines the class based shooter aspect that I loved from TF2 with the battle royale genre. There is also a plethora of guns that are fun to use. I normally don't like battle royale games but this game has me hooked because of it's fast paced shooting with different usable characters that have different sets of skills. Game's Wikipedia
